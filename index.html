Hi there, I'm Bogdan Ivanyuk-Skulskyi

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at CRIStAL, University of Lille and Cyclope.ai (Vinci Autoroutes), advised by prof. Nadiya Shvai, Amir Nakib, and El-ghazali Talbi. Prior to joining CRIStAL, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in 2020 and Master's degree in 2022 at National Univerisy of Kyiv-Mohyla Academy, where I worked under the supervision of prof. Galyna Kriukova, Andrii Dmytryshyn, and Nadiya Shvai.

During my Masters I worked at Samsung Research on voice security applications and at University of Toronto, advised by Dr. Brokoslaw Laschowski, and Dr. Alex Mihailidis on video classification model for exoskeleton control.

My research interests primarily include anomaly detection, video processing.

Publications:
